# Code of Conduct

We follow the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) to ensure everyone can learn and ship safely.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful, patient, and assume positive intent.
- Offer constructive feedback; focus on ideas, not people.
- Share credit, document changes, and keep the project transparent.
- Avoid harassment, trolling, or publishing private information.

## Enforcement
- Report unacceptable behavior to the maintainers at `security@cybersec-toolkit.local`.
- All reports are reviewed promptly and kept confidential.
- Maintainers may remove or block contributors who violate this policy.
